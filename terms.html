Description of Service

Dukan Daftar is a shop management application designed for small and medium retail businesses in Pakistan. The app provides the following core services:

📦 Core Features
✓
Point of Sale (POS) — quick sale and transaction recording
✓
Credit Book (Khata) — customer credit and debt tracking
✓
Inventory management — product catalog, stock levels, barcode scanning
✓
Supplier management and purchase order tracking
✓
Expense tracking and financial reports
✓
Data backup — local storage and optional Google Drive cloud backup
✓
Thermal receipt printing (Enterprise plan — with compatible hardware)
