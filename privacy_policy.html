1
Information We Collect

Dukan Daftar is designed as an offline-first application. The vast majority of data you enter into the app stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

📱 Data You Enter Into the App (Stored Locally Only)
Shop name, owner name, and contact details (setup information)
Product names, prices, barcodes, and stock quantities
Customer names, phone numbers, and credit balances
Supplier names, contact info, and purchase records
Sales transactions, receipts, and daily records
Expense records and categories
PIN lock code (stored locally, encrypted)
📊 Data We May Collect Automatically (Anonymized)
Crash reports and error logs (to fix bugs — contains no personal data)
General app usage analytics (screens visited, features used — no business data)
Device type and Android version (for compatibility improvements)

We never collect your actual business data, customer information, transaction amounts, or any shop-specific content.

2
How We Use Your Information

The limited anonymized data we may collect is used exclusively to:

Fix bugs and crashes reported through automated crash reporting
Improve app performance and user experience
Understand which features are most useful to plan future updates
Ensure compatibility with different Android device models

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating and improving the app.

3
Data Storage & Security

All your business data is stored locally on your device using the device's secure local storage. This means:

Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to back it up
No internet connection is required to use the core features of the app
Even without internet (load shedding, no SIM), the app works completely normally
Your PIN is stored in encrypted form — we cannot access it
Uninstalling the app will delete all local data from the device
🔒
Security Note: We strongly recommend setting a PIN lock and taking regular local backups. If your phone is lost or reset, local data may be unrecoverable unless you have a backup saved to Google Drive or your device storage.
4
Google Drive Backup

Dukan Daftar offers an optional Google Drive backup feature available in our Enterprise plan. Here is exactly how it works:

☁️ How Google Drive Backup Works
You must explicitly connect your Google account to enable this feature
Your backup file is saved to your own Google Drive account — not ours
We do not have access to your Google Drive or the backup files
The backup contains an encrypted copy of your local database
You can delete your backup from Google Drive at any time
You can disconnect your Google account from the app at any time in Settings

Google Drive is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy (policies.google.com). By using the Google Drive backup feature, you agree to Google's Terms of Service in addition to ours.

5
Third-Party Services

Dukan Daftar may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Google Drive API — for optional cloud backup (Enterprise plan only). Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting and stability improvements. Collects anonymized crash data only, no personal information.
Google Play Services — for app distribution and basic Android services.

We do not use any advertising networks, social media trackers, or marketing analytics tools in the app.

6
Permissions We Request

Dukan Daftar requests the following Android permissions and here is exactly why each one is needed:

Camera — to scan product barcodes for quick sale and inventory updates. No photos are captured or stored by the app.
Storage / File Access — to save local backup files to your device's storage so you can later restore your data.
Internet — only used for Google Drive backup sync (optional) and crash reporting. Not required for daily use.
Bluetooth — to connect with Bluetooth thermal receipt printers (Enterprise plan only).
Vibration — for haptic feedback on barcode scan success.

You can revoke any permission at any time through your Android device settings. Revoking camera permission will disable barcode scanning. Revoking storage permission will disable local backup.

7
Children's Privacy

Dukan Daftar is a business management application intended for use by adults operating shops and retail businesses. It is not directed at or designed for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through our app, please contact us at info@dukandaftar.pk and we will promptly address the situation.

8
Your Rights & Choices

Since your business data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it at all times:

Access: All your data is visible and accessible within the app at any time.
Export: You can export your data as a backup file at any time from Settings → Backup.
Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. You can also clear individual records from within the app.
Opt out of analytics: You can disable crash reporting and analytics in Settings → Privacy.
Google Drive disconnect: You can disconnect your Google account at any time in Settings → Backup → Google Drive.
9
Data Retention & Deletion

Local data: Your business data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed and you choose to keep it. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data permanently.

Google Drive backups: Backup files in your Google Drive are retained until you delete them manually from your Google Drive account or disconnect the app.

Crash reports: Anonymized crash data is automatically deleted from our analytics systems after 90 days.

We do not retain any identifiable user data on our servers because we do not have servers that hold your personal or business data.
